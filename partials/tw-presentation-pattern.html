TW Presentation Pattern

tw-presentation-pattern is a simple attribute based directive that formats test entered into controls.

<input type="text"
  class="form-control"
  ng-model="{{vm.model.presentationPattern}}"
  ng-minlength="5"
  ng-maxlength="9"
  tw-presentation-pattern="2-3-4"
  tw-presentation-separator="-"
  tw-validation
  tw-focusable />
