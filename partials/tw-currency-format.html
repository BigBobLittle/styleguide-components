TW Currenct Format

tw-currency-format works alongside ng-model to format currency values into their local form.

{{vm.currencyFormat.currency1}}
{{vm.currencyFormat.currency2}}

    tw-currency-format="{{vm.currencyFormat.currency1}}" />

</div>
